What is Trackr? Personal Data Toolkit

Trackr is an app that gives you a single place to keep track of anything about you, your life, habits, and more.

You can use Trackr to log your eating and drinking habits, whether you made it to the gym, your mood, and anything else you can think of.

Once you've logged enough data, you can visualize the relationships between your habits, your moods, and activities! Maybe you feel anxious when you have too much coffee, or restless and depressed when you don't make it to the gym. Trackr gives you the tools to gain insights about your life!
